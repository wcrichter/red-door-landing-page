Advocacy

More Than a Tool on "The Hill"

Advocacy is no longer just a tactic used in political campaigns; we’ve moved beyond “pass a bill; kill a bill.” So what does that mean? Today, it is about solving issues and affecting change – inspiring collaboration between nontraditional allies who, even though they may not agree on everything, are coming together to accomplish mutually beneficial goals. It’s highlighting issues and supporting causes; it’s integrating advocacy tactics with marketing strategies designed to influence decision makers and to change local, state, and federal policy and practice. That’s the differentiator.

Leveraging a New Set of Tools And Strategies

In my twenty years of experience developing advocacy campaigns and working with organizations, ranging from not-for-profits to Fortune 500 companies, we’ve seen emerging trends that are shaping today’s advocacy landscape. Done well, advocacy can further your organization’s efforts to achieve its goals and objectives – making it an essential resource in every toolbox.
